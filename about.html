About REEX

Boutique real estate agency redefining cross-border investment excellence

Our Story

Situated at the crossroads of Southeast Asia's most dynamic real estate corridors, REEX is a Batam-based boutique real estate agency with a bold, global outlook, built on passion, expertise and global vision. We specialize in helping discerning investors - both local and foreign to navigate property opportunities not only in Batam, but also in Singapore, Malaysia (Johor) and the Middle East (especially in Dubai). Our mission is to bridge borders, deliver insight and craft paths to maximize value, security and long-term satisfaction.

We are the nexus where local opportunity and international investments converge. We serve clients who are keen not only to capitalize on Batam's growing real-estate potential, but also to diversify their portfolios across global hotspots like Singapore, Malaysia and UAE.

Why Batam as a Home Base

Batam's strategic location of just 20 - 45km from Singapore and close to Malaysia makes it a natural investment hub. It offers a rare blend of lower land and property costs compared to neighbouring markets as well as robust infrastructure development and an increasingly favorable regulations for foreign investors under recent reforms. Batam is emerging not just as a gateway, but as a place whose own real estate market delivers steady rental yields, resilient housing demand and high potential growth.

Vision Forward

We believe that the next decade will see even greater convergence among Southeast Asia's property markets, with Singapore, Johor and Batam forming an increasingly integrated "triangle" of investments, commuter flows, cross-border trade and regulatory coordination. Meanwhile, Dubai offers the pulse of the global market with dynamic, high value and diversified. By serving all of these, our clients gain not just property assets, but resilience.

If you are an investor who seeks more than just bricks and mortar, someone who wants clarity, strategy and lasting value, REEX welcomes you. Let us help you unlock opportunity, wherever you see it.

Why Our Clients Trust Us

Confidentiality

We maintain the highest standards of client confidentiality and data protection, ensuring your investment activities and personal information remain secure and private.

Cross-Border Risk Mitigation

We've helped Singaporeans, Malaysians, Indonesians and Middle Eastern Investors mitigate risks of cross-border property ownership, especially in navigating minimum price thresholds, state consents, ownership rights and tax implications. For instance, foreign investors planning to purchase properties in the international zones of Johor often require minimum purchase prices of RM 1 million+ for high rise and RM 2 million+ for landed).

Nimble and Proactive

We are nimble and proactive. When Batam's Job Creation Law and related regulation eased foreign property ownership, we were among the first advisers to integrate those into our guidance and deal structuring.
